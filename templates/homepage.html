What happens when you ask an AI to draw famous paintings as simple computer graphics? This gallery shows how language models turn famous artworks into SVG images. Even though these AIs aren't trained to make vector graphics, it's fun to see how they mix what they know about art with their ability to write SVG code. This project is inspired by Simon Willison's practice of using this as a creative LLM benchmark.
